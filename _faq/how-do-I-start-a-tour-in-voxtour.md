**Q:** How do I start a tour in VoxTour?

**A:** Starting a tour in VoxTour.ai is simple and flexible. Follow these steps:

1. Search for a city or destination
2. Select a tour from the available list
3. Open the tour page
4. Tap or click **Start Tour**
5. Choose a stop and tap **Visit** to begin listening

You can start from any stop and explore tours in any order.
