**Q:** Do I need to create an account to use VoxTour?

**A:** No, you do not need to create an account to explore VoxTour.ai. You can browse cities, view tours, and preview points of interest without signing in.

However, an account is required if you want to:

- Start listening to audio stories
- Save your progress
- Add tours and places to your favorites
- Access your content across multiple devices

Creating an account is quick and free.
