**Q:** How do I change the language?

**A:** You can change the language of the entire VoxTour.ai application in the Settings menu.

To change the application language:

1. Open your profile menu
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Select **Language**
4. Choose your preferred language

You can also change the language of a specific tour or point of interest (POI) while viewing tour details or a POI page by selecting the available audio language.
