Find Your City & Tours

Find Your City & Tours

VoxTour.ai lets you explore cities in a flexible way.
You can discover curated tours or explore individual points of interest (POIs) directly from the map.

You can browse cities, tours, and places without signing in.
To listen to audio stories, you‚Äôll need to sign in.

Step 1: Search for a city

On desktop:
On the home page, use the Search by destination field to enter a city name (for example, Rome or Paris), then select it from the results.

On mobile:
Tap the search field, type a city name, and select it from the list.

Step 2: Explore the city map

After selecting a city, you‚Äôll see:

  • a map with numbered and icon-based POIs
  • a list of available tours for that city

The map and the tour list work together, you can explore visually or browse tours first.

Step 3: Explore individual places (POIs)

You don‚Äôt have to follow a tour to explore.

Tap or click any POI icon on the map to open a place preview.
Each POI preview shows:

  • place name and category

  • available audio languages
  • a short description

This is ideal if you want to:

  • explore freely
  • listen only to places nearby
  • stop and play audio whenever something catches your eye

Step 4: Browse tours in the list

The tour list shows curated routes created to help you explore a city around a theme or area.

Each tour card includes:

  • Tour title
  • Rating
  • Estimated duration
  • Total distance
  • Number of stops (locations)

These details help you quickly compare tours and choose one that fits your time and walking preferences.

Step 5: Check available languages

Next to the tour rating, you may see one or more language icons (for example üá∫üá∏ üá´üá∑ üáÆüáπ).

Step 6: Preview the tour route on the map

Tours include a route icon (map/book icon) next to the Open button.

Tap or click this icon to:

  • display the full tour route on the map
  • see how all tour stops are connected
  • understand the walking path before starting

This is helpful if you want to check distances, walking direction, or how the tour fits into your plans.

Step 7: Open a tour

Select Open to view the full tour page.

From there, you can:

  • see all included locations
  • check detailed tour information
  • choose your listening language
  • start the tour after signing in

Choose how you want to explore

Start & Listen to a Tour

Start & Listen to a Tour

Once you choose a tour, VoxTour.ai makes it easy to preview the route, review stops, and start listening at your own pace.

Step 1: Open the tour page

From the city screen, tap or click Open on any tour card.

The tour page shows:

  • Tour overview and description
  • Rating and available audio languages
  • Estimated walking distance and duration
  • Number of included stops
  • Full list of Points of Interest (POIs)

Step 2: Choose your listening language

On the tour page, select your preferred audio language.

Tap or click the language selector next to the Start Tour button.
Available languages may include English, Hebrew, Russian, and others depending on the tour.

Step 3: Open a tour and view stops

Tap or click Start Tour (or open a tour page).

If you are not signed in, VoxTour.ai will prompt you to sign in or create an account before continuing.

Opening a tour does not start audio automatically.
Instead, VoxTour.ai displays the list of stops (locations) included in the tour.

Each stop displays:

  • Stop number and name
  • Photo preview
  • Visit button (opens location details and audio story)
  • Direction button (opens navigation to the stop)

You can explore stops in order or jump to any location.

Step 4: Preview the tour route on the map

On mobile:

Tours include a Route icon (map/book icon). Tapping the Route icon opens the map centered on your current location.

This means:

  • If you are currently in a different country, the route may not be immediately visible.
  • You may need to manually zoom or move the map to view the full route.

The same behavior applies when tapping Direction - navigation is based on your current location.

On desktop:

The map appears alongside the tour content.

This makes it easier to:

  • View the entire tour route
  • Understand stop locations
  • Explore the path even if you are not physically in the destination city

Step 5: Start listening at a stop

To begin listening:

Tap or click Visit next to a stop.

When you select Visit, VoxTour.ai:

  • Opens the stop page
  • Launches the audio player
  • Starts the story for that location

You can start from any stop, you do not have to follow the route in order.

Step 6: Use directions to navigate

The Direction button helps you navigate to a stop using map navigation.

On mobile:

  • Directions may open navigation based on your current location.

On desktop:

  • You can preview the stop location and route on the map before traveling.

Step 7: Use the audio player

While listening, you can:

  • Pause or resume playback
  • Skip forward or backward between stops
  • Adjust playback speed
  • Continue exploring the map while listening

You can stop and resume the tour anytime.

Step 8: Explore stops during the tour

While following the tour, you can:

  • Open the map to see upcoming stops
  • Select stops manually
  • Read descriptions and view images
  • Use navigation directions between locations

Flexible listening experience

With VoxTour.ai, you can:

  • Follow the tour route step-by-step
  • Visit stops in any order
  • Pause and continue later
  • Combine guided tours with spontaneous exploration

Tip:
Before starting a tour, preview the route on desktop or zoom out on mobile to better understand the full walking path.

Account Management

Managing your account profile is quick and straightforward. Follow these steps to edit your personal details like your first name, last name, nickname, and birth year.

How to Access the Profile Edit Page

  1. Log in to your account.

  2. Navigate to the Profile section.

  3. Click on the Edit Profile icon (the pencil icon) located near your profile picture.

Editing Your Profile Information

On the Edit Profile page, you can modify the following fields:

  • First Name: Update your first name as it will appear on your profile.
  • Last Name: Change your last name to reflect updated information.
  • Nickname: Add or edit your nickname for a more personalized touch.
  • Birth Year: Adjust your birth year to ensure your profile is accurate.

[]

Saving Changes

  1. After updating the necessary fields, click the Save button at the top right corner of the page.
  2. Your changes will be saved immediately, and your profile will be updated.

Viewing Updated Profile

  1. Once saved, you will be automatically redirected to the Profile page where you can verify the updated information in your profile summary.
  2. Verify the updated information in your profile summary, which includes your name and other relevant details.

Additional Notes

  • Profile Picture: You can also update your profile picture by clicking the pencil icon over your avatar.
  • Some fields may not be editable depending on your account type or permissions.
  • If you encounter any issues while editing your profile, contact our support team for assistance.

By following the steps above, you can easily manage your account details to ensure your profile stays accurate and up-to-date.

Settings

Settings

VoxTour.ai allows you to manage your account credentials, language preferences, and profile settings easily from the Settings menu.

Settings are available on both mobile and desktop, and the functionality is the same across platforms.

Step 1: Open Settings

On mobile:

  1. Tap your profile avatar in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap Settings.

On desktop:

  1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner.
  2. Select Settings from the dropdown menu.

Step 2: Open General Settings

Inside the Settings menu, tap or click General.

This section contains your account credentials, language preferences, and account management options.

Credentials

The Credentials section displays your registered email address and allows you to update login details.

Change email address

  1. Tap or click Change email
  2. Enter:
    • New email address
    • Confirm email address
    • Current password
  3. Tap or click Save

If you do not remember your password, sign out and use password reset.

Change password

  1. Tap or click Change password
  2. Enter:
    • Current password
    • New password
    • Confirm new password
  3. Tap or click Save

Social Sign-In Status

This section shows how your account is connected.

Example:
