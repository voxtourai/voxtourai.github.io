How to create an account (Sign up)

Creating an account in VoxTour.ai is quick and only takes a few steps.
An account is required to start listening to audio tours.

Step 1: Open the sign-up page

On mobile:
From the home screen, tap the menu icon ☰ in the top corner and select Sign Up.

On desktop:
On the home page, click Sign Up in the top-right corner of the screen.

Step 2: Enter your details

To create a new account, sign up using your email address.

Fill in the following fields:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Confirm password

Make sure your email address is correct — it will be used to verify your account.

When finished, tap or click Get Started.

Step 3: Verify your email address

After submitting the form, VoxTour.ai will send a 6-digit verification code to your email address.

On mobile and desktop:

  1. Check your inbox for the verification email.
  2. Enter the verification code on the screen.
  3. Tap or click Verify.

If you don’t see the email, check your spam folder or select Resend Code.

Step 4: Account created

Once your email is verified, your account is ready to use.

You can now:

  • Sign in to your account
  • Start listening to audio tours
  • Save your progress
  • Add places and tours to your favorites

Note:
Google and Apple are available for sign in only.
If you already have an account, choose Sign In instead of creating a new one.

How to sign in

If you already have a VoxTour.ai account, you can sign in using your email and password or a supported third-party option, depending on your device.

Step 1: Open the sign-in page

On mobile:
From the home screen, tap the menu icon ☰ and select Sign In.

On desktop:
On the home page, click Sign In in the top-right corner of the screen.

Step 2: Choose how to sign in

VoxTour.ai offers different sign-in options depending on your platform.

On desktop (web)

You can sign in using:

  • Email and password
  • Google

Enter your email and password, then click Sign in,
or select Continue with Google.

On iPhone (iOS app)

You can sign in using:

  • Email and password
  • Apple
  • Google

Choose your preferred option:

  • Enter your email and password and tap Sign in
  • Or tap Continue with Apple
  • Or tap Continue with Google

On Android (app)

You can sign in using:

  • Email and password
  • Google

Enter your email and password and tap Sign in,
or tap Continue with Google.

Step 3: Forgot your password?

If you don’t remember your password:

  1. Select Did you forget your password?
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password

Step 4: Signed in and ready to go

Once signed in, you can:

  • Start listening to audio tours
  • Continue where you left off
  • Save your favorite places and tours
  • Access your account across devices

Note:
If you don’t have an account yet, select Sign up to create one.
